Зарубежные сообщества Шадоводов и VLXer'ов в частности

What is the VLX? The letters themselves stand for "V-Twin, Lowered, eXtended rake." Unless you happen to have a Japanese model Steed, they're tagged "VLS" with the difference being a Springer front end. What does that mean in the real world? Pure middle-weight perfection. Good looks, charm, and grace wrapped up in a stylin' & profilin' classic chopper-esque style. Simply put, it's the Coolest in the Shadows. "VLXcellence and VLXuberance" that allows you to Ride with Pride like only a VLXer can. Be warned however, make fun of a VLX and get stabbed in the liver...
